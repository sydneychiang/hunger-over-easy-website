About Us

Hunger, Over Easy is a five hour cooking celebration that showcases a variety of activities including a "Chopped" style cooking competition, educational booths about nutrition, food challenges, in addition to service booths where we help make food for the homeless.

All of our proceeds are donated to Meals on Wheels America, the oldest and largest national organization federally supported to meet the nutritional and social needs of seniors. They support more than 5,000 community-based senior nutrition programs across the United States.
